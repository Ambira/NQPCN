About Us

Network for Quality, Productivity, and Competitiveness – Nepal (NQPCN) is a network of Entrepreneurs, Managers, Academicians, and Consultants of Nepal. Established in 2004, NQPCN aims to enhance competitiveness through sharing, learning, and applying globally developed quality and productivity tools and techniques for social and economic development within the nation.

NQPCN is a not-for-profit organization solely run from membership fees and voluntary contributions. Our mission is to foster collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and continuous improvement to help drive national growth and development.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create, share, and learn knowledge and experience on quality and productivity to enhance the competitiveness of Nepal. By applying globally recognized tools and techniques, we aim to empower individuals, organizations, and society as a whole.

Mission and Vision

Mission: NQPCN aims at enhancing the competitiveness of individuals, organizations, and society as a whole through quality and productivity, contributing to national economic development.

Vision: To establish a functional national and international network of individuals and organizations working in the areas of quality, productivity, and competitiveness. We aim to sustain this network as a not-for-profit civil society institution.

Our Core Values

  • Quality: We are committed to promoting learning and practices that ensure high standards of quality in every aspect of our work.
  • Productivity: We support each other in productive endeavors, continuously striving to increase efficiency and results.
  • Competitiveness: Through mutual support and collaboration, we enhance the competitiveness of Nepal’s economy and society.
  • Collaboration: We work together with national and international partners to share knowledge and resources for mutual growth.

Our Activities

  • Creating knowledge on quality, productivity, and competitiveness themes through study and research.
  • Organizing learning-sharing forums each month among Network members.
  • Sharing resources on Quality, Productivity, and Competitiveness through electronic media (websites) and print media (articles, books).
  • Participating in national and international conferences as delegates and resource persons.
  • Networking with national and international forums on quality, productivity, and competitiveness.
  • Collaborating on seminars and conventions with regional and international forums on quality and productivity.
