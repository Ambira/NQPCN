Established in 2004, our goal is to enhance competitiveness through sharing, learning, and applying globally developed quality and productivity tools and techniques.

Our Goal

Create, share, and learn the knowledge and experience on quality and productivity to enhance competitiveness in Nepal.

Mission and Vision

Mission: NQPCN aims to enhance the competitiveness of individuals, organizations, and society as a whole through quality and productivity.

Vision: Instituting a functional national and international network of individuals and organizations working in quality, productivity, and competitiveness, and sustaining it as a not-for-profit civil society institution.

Our Activities

  • Creating knowledge on quality, productivity, and competitiveness themes through study and research.
  • Organizing learning-sharing forums among Network members each month.
  • Sharing resources on Quality, Productivity, and Competitiveness via electronic media (websites) and print media (articles, books).
  • Participating in national and international conferences as delegates and resource persons.
  • Networking with national and international forums on quality, productivity, and competitiveness.
  • Collaborating on seminars and conventions with regional and international forums on quality and productivity.
